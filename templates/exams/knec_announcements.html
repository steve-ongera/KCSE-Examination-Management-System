2023 KCSE Examination Results Release

Results • December 18, 2023
High Priority

Official release of Kenya Certificate of Secondary Education results.

The Kenya National Examinations Council is pleased to announce the release of the 2023 KCSE examination results.

Key Dates:

  • Results available online: December 20, 2023
  • School result slips collection: December 21-22, 2023
  • Appeals window: December 20 - January 10, 2024
All candidates can access their results through the KNEC portal using their index numbers.
Posted by: KNEC Public Relations Office

2024 KCPE Registration Deadline Extension

Registration • December 15, 2023
Medium Priority

Extended deadline for KCPE candidate registration.

KNEC has extended the registration deadline for the 2024 KCPE examinations by two weeks.

New deadline: March 15, 2024

All headteachers are advised to ensure:

  1. Complete registration of all candidates
  2. Verification of candidate details
  3. Payment of all examination fees
Late registration will attract additional charges.
Posted by: KNEC Registration Department
